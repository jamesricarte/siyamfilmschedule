INT. DAY - MAYA’S BEDROOM


Maya’s room is full of medals, books, journals, and art materials. Things that reflect her hobbies and personality. She is sleeping soundly on the bed. She wakes up with the sound of the church bell. She gets up to get ready for her choir practice.

She sits by her vanity table, fully dressed up. She combs her hair as she hums to the Marian song “Stella Maris”. She is wearing a white dress, pairing it with dainty sandals, and gold jewelry. She looks pristine, clean, and presentable, as she scans herself in the mirror again.

11. INT. DINING AREA - NIGHT


Maya enters the dining area. Her mother, Gloria, is setting up the table after cooking dinner. They sit down, pray, and enjoy their dinner. They are chatting as they eat their meal.

MAYA (V.O.)

Mahal na mahal ko ang mama. Siya nalang ang pamilya ko, kaya lagi ko siyang sinusunod.

As they are conversing with each other over their meal, Gloria’s tone suddenly rises. Her tone is a bit mad Maya.

GLORIA
Oy! Ikaw Maya ha. Parang napapadalas ang labas mo. Araw araw na yan ay!

MAYA (pouting)
Mama, school, trabaho, Simbahan, at bahay lang naman pinupuntahan ko.

Gloria’s face is still doubting.

MAYA (CONT’D)
Lagi lang talaga ako busy sa school, Ma! Tapos lagi ba night shift sa karinderya tapos may choir practice pa.

GLORIA
Ay basta! Hindi ko gusto na lagi ka nalang nasa labas. Delikado ang panahon ngayon, dito ka lang lagi sa bahay.

Maya feels defeated and accepts her mother’s command.

MAYA
Opo, ma. Maya lowers her head and continues eating. Her mother’s voice echoes in her head. GLORIA (echoes) Ayaw ko nang layas ka ng layas. Baka nababarkada ka na ha! Bawal ang boyfriend, sinasabi ko sa’yo, Maya! Hindi kita lagi nakikita baka ano-ano nang ginagawa mo! Gloria’s voice slowly fades in Maya’s ears. MAYA (V.O.) Maya ang pangalan ko ngunit tila ba’y hindi ako nakalalaya sa kulungan. Sana isang araw ako ay tulad na ng maya-maya na nakakalipad ng malaya. Hindi ko alam kung bakit ang lugar na tinatawag kong tahanan ay malungkot, at nakakatakot.
